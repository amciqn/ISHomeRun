History of the Home Run



The first ever Home Run was hit by Ross Barnes of the Chicago Whitestockings (Later known as the Cubs) in 1876. Rule changes in 1920 allowed hitters such as Babe Ruth and Rogers Hornsby to hit the baseball past the fence in the outfield starting a rise in popularity of the Home Run. Nowadays the Home Run is very common. In May of 2019 1,135 Home Runs were hit the most ever in a month in the history of the MLB
